# Travel Policies and Visa Requirements

## Japan Visa Requirements

### UAE Passport Holders
UAE passport holders can enter Japan visa-free for up to 30 days for tourism purposes. Your passport must be valid for at least 6 months beyond your intended stay. No visa application is required for short-term tourist visits.

### Indian Passport Holders
Indian citizens require a visa to enter Japan. Tourist visas are typically valid for 15 to 30 days. You must apply through the Japanese embassy or consulate with the following documents:
- Valid passport (minimum 6 months validity)
- Completed visa application form
- Recent passport-size photograph
- Flight itinerary
- Hotel reservations
- Bank statements (last 6 months)
- Employment certificate

Processing time is typically 5-7 business days.

### US Passport Holders
US citizens can enter Japan visa-free for tourism or business stays up to 90 days. Passport must be valid for the duration of your stay.

### UK Passport Holders
UK passport holders can visit Japan visa-free for up to 90 days for tourism or business purposes.

## United States Visa Requirements

### UAE Passport Holders
UAE citizens need a B-1/B-2 visa for tourism to the United States. Apply through the US Embassy:
- Valid passport
- DS-160 form
- Visa interview appointment
- Visa fee payment
- Supporting documents (employment, financial statements)

### Indian Passport Holders
Indian citizens require a B-1/B-2 tourist visa. The process is similar to UAE requirements. Additionally, ESTA is not available; you must apply for a full visa.

## Refund and Cancellation Policies

### Refundable Tickets
Refundable tickets can be canceled up to 48 hours before departure, subject to a 10% processing fee. The remaining amount will be refunded to your original payment method within 7-10 business days.

To request a refund:
1. Contact the airline or booking agent at least 48 hours before departure
2. Provide booking reference and passenger details
3. Complete refund request form
4. Processing fee will be deducted automatically

### Non-Refundable Tickets
Non-refundable tickets cannot be canceled for a cash refund. However, many airlines offer the option to:
- Change flight dates (subject to change fees and fare difference)
- Request travel credit for future use (typically valid for 1 year)
- Purchase travel insurance at booking for potential coverage

### Same-Day Cancellations
Cancellations made within 24 hours of booking may be eligible for a full refund regardless of ticket type, per airline policy. This varies by carrier and region.

## Layover Policies

### Overnight Layovers
If your layover exceeds 8 hours overnight (between 10 PM and 6 AM), you may need accommodation. Some airlines provide:
- Complimentary hotel stays for layovers over 12 hours
- Transit hotel vouchers
- Lounge access

Contact your airline to confirm layover assistance policies.

### Transit Visas
Some countries require transit visas even if you're not leaving the airport:
- **China**: Required for layovers over 24 hours (some cities offer 72-hour visa-free transit)
- **United States**: Generally not required if you have a valid visa or ESTA
- **United Kingdom**: Not required for airside transit
- **Schengen Area**: Generally not required for airside transit under 24 hours

Always verify with the embassy of your layover country.

## Travel Insurance

We strongly recommend purchasing comprehensive travel insurance that covers:
- Medical emergencies and evacuation
- Trip cancellation and interruption
- Lost or delayed baggage
- Flight delays and missed connections

Insurance can typically be purchased during booking or separately through third-party providers.

Entry requirements may change based on current health situations. Always check:
- Vaccination requirements
- COVID-19 testing requirements (PCR or antigen tests)
- Quarantine regulations
- Health declaration forms

Visit official government websites or contact airlines for the most current information before traveling.

## Passport Validity Rules

Most countries require your passport to be valid for:
- **6 months** beyond your intended departure date (most common)
- **3 months** for some European countries
- At least 1-2 blank pages for stamps

Check specific country requirements before booking.
